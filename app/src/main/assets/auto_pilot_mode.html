Auto Pilot Mode is an automated system that redeems and applies the J Tickets by itself at regular intervals. The redemption and application highly depends on your Average Play Per Day (APD)

Redeem engine:

  • At Regular intervals, the redeem engine will start redeeming J Tickets, starting from the lowest value J Vouchers.

  • It won’t redeem J Tickets above the APD numbered J Voucher and repeat the cycle again starting from the lowest value J Voucher, as per the availability of the credentia currency (cc).

Example:

If a Player has an APD of Rs. 28, J Vouchers numbered less than or equal to 28 will only be redeemed in auto pilot mode.

i.e., J5, J11, J25 will be redeemed, J55 won’t be redeemed as the number is higher than 28. And if there’s cc available after redeeming one set of these J Vouchers, the redeem engine will repeat the redeem cycle again from J5, followed by J11, so on and so forth.

Apply engine:

  • Once the J Ticket is redeemed, it will get applied automatically at regular intervals.

  • X number tickets of each J Voucher will be applied at Regular intervals.

  • System will keep applying J tickets only until the Maximum Apply Potential per day.

  • Maximum Apply Potential per day (in cc) = Average play per day (in Rs). Example: If a players APD is Rs.70, then his Maximum Apply Potential will be CC 70

Example:

Let’s assume a player has a total of 46.92 CC worth J tickets redeemed

i.e., the player has 6 J Tickets each of the following J Vouchers waiting to be applied

J5 Redeemed @0.91 cc so 6 JT x 0.91 = 5.46 cc worth J5

J11 Redeemed @1.91 cc so 6 JT x 1.91 = 11.46 cc worth J11

J25 Redeemed @5.0 cc so 6 JT x 5.0 = 30 cc worth J25

So here total worth of available J Tickets is 46.92cc

Now the APD is Rs.28, so the Maximum Apply Potential “MAP” is “28 cc / day”

As per MAP the following J Tickets will be applied-

6 (J5) worth 5.46 cc +

6 (J11) worth 11.46 cc +

2 (J25) worth 10 cc

That’s total worth of 26.92 cc J Tickets

The remaining 4 (J25) will not get applied, because 6 (J5) + 6 (J11) + 2 (J25) = 26.92 and Maximum Apply Potential is 28 cc only.

  • Balance Apply potential = Maximum Apply Potential – Net worth of Already applied JTicket (in cc)

So, after the apply hourly cycle, balance of apply potential will be 28cc – 26.92cc = 1.08 cc
